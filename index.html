About

My name is Rijo George and this is the place where I spend my free time. I am a software engineer by profession and have worked on many interesting products. I started the habit of reading books three years back. It was difficult at first but now it's more of a favourite thing to do. I love to read both fiction and non-fiction which makes me think about life, and all its little miracles.

I was initially posting all my thoughts on twitter, but twitter as a medium doesn't excite me anymore. I want to write down my thoughts and ideas without restrictions and barriers. Twitter gives me anxiety attacks and has a feeling of being watched. Every word I write needs to be best because there are people watching you. But here I am in control. This is my space and I have the freedom to write whatever comes to my mind.​
